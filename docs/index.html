How it stays honest
The app loads pre-bundled snapshots so there are no live-API waits, and leads with transparent, well-sourced methods. Cover is an index, not a census — NEON percent cover is an ocular, relative estimate over overlapping layers, so it is read as composition, never as exact area. Diversity uses Hill numbers for one continuous family from richness through evenness, and extends richness with Chao2, an incidence-based estimator over the nested subplots, reported with its confidence interval rather than a bare count. Climate & phenology correlations are tested against a permutation null on a matched-n sample, so a site with only a few survey years is told its record is too short to claim a trend, not handed a spurious one. The unknown-nativity rate is published alongside every native-vs-introduced split. Educational data-exploration tools, not affiliated with NEON, Battelle, or the NSF.
